In the folder where you run The Sandbox.exe must be SDL.dll

To select a block, press on the keyboard:
1 - for stone,
2 - for sand,
3 - for water,
4 - for emptiness

To fill a square or rectangle with the selected block, you need 2 clicks on the left mouse button
1 - in the upper left corner of the square or rectangle
2 - in the lower right corner of this square or rectangle

Press the space bar on your keyboard to generate a map
